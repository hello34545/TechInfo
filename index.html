Best Specs For Home And Gaming Use

If you are planing to buy a new PC then we have the best specs for you!

PROCESSORS Processors are very important parts of the computer which processes the information of the computer. The best processors for home use are i5 or i3. If you are going to use it for gaming then i7 or i9.

For RAM you should get at least 8gb for home use and for gaming 16gb is enough for smoothe lag free gaming.

  • A good powersupply is vital for a good PC for both home and gaming use. Some of the best powersupply are Corsair CX450, Corsair RM550x (2021), XPG Core Reactor 650W.
  • For a faster pc you should consider buying a ssd rather then a hdd as it faster.
  • If you are going to get gaming PC and want good graphics you should buy NVIDIA GTX 3090 OR GTX 3090 TI.
